About

How an English and Theater background brought me here.

  • 1992 - 2001

    Gaming Changed My Life

    For as long as I can remember, I loved video games. From the first time I picked up a Sega Genesis controller to play Sonic the Hedgehog, to getting my very first Nintendo console, the Gamecube in 2001, my passion for the creation, technology, and design of these games were born.

  • 2008 - 2010

    My Career Begins in Computers

    I was hired as a member of All Jersey Studios in high school, where my job was to shoot, edit, and ship video for NJ events including Weddings, Birthdays, Graduations, and various Corporate functions. I worked with Adobe Premiere and their suite of products, picking up best practices and creative direction as I went along. Coding is a lot like video editing, in that there is a way to make it all look good without sacrificing your personal touch.

  • 2010 - 2014

    Education at Rutgers University

    At Rutgers, I studied classic literature and writing and earned a Bachelor's Degree. I acted in plays, wrote my own, and hosted the campus-wide morning TV show, which also included Freshman Orientation to tens of thousands of students and families.At Rutgers Television, I furthered my video editing and computer skills as a responsibility to my fellow students. I learned how to be sociable and engaging, and connected with people of all backgrounds, this as being crucial to strong web services.

  • 2014 - 2017

    Selling My Ideas and Myself

    My skills in technology and communication led me to Macmillan Learning to begin my career post-college. I worked with thousands of professors and university figures across Pennsylvania, Texas, Minnesota, Wisconsin, Arizona, West Virginia, Maine, Idaho, Connecticut, and Massachusetts to ensure their students used our tech and services to increase grades and graduation rates. Over $4,000,000 of business later, our influence on effective student learning is boundless.

  • 2018

    A Career in Development

    In January, I earned a Coding Certificate from my Alma Mater. With years of sales experience behind me, I look forward to working with and for people like me, who love great ideas and forward-moving technologies, and being able to communicate all of these in a professional, high quality manner.
