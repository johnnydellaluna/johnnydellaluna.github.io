Crash's Crystal Collector

Help Crash Bandicoot collect his crystals in order to stop Cortex's new nefarious plan! Your goal is to match the assigned number by clicking on the crystals to add points. Each of the 4 crystals have different values, and they will only be displayed once you click on them, so choose wisely. Their values are randomized at the start of every game. Go over the target score, and it's game over. Good luck!
